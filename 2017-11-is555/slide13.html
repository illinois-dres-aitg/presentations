Issues in IT Accessibility

  • Human resources: There are not enough people who undestand the experience of disability and the technqiues to make resources more accessible.
  • Roles: No one needs to know everything, but everyone needs to know something to contribute to accessibility.
  • Authoring: Authoring tools need to support accessibility by default, rather than by exception.
  • Standards: Standards need to support accessibility and provide a means to make it easier to make resources more accessible.
