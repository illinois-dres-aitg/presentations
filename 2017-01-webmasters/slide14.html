DRES Badging Training

Accessibility Badging (Starting in March 2017)

  • Courses will be offered through CITL
  • 4-8 hours of synchoronous online training per badge
  • Compentency based testing of skills
  • Support learning HTML5 and ARIA techniques to meet WCAG 2.0 Level A and AA requirements
  • Proposed Badges organized by Rule Categories:
    • Web Accessibility Evaluation: Basic
    • Web Accessibility Evaluation: Advanced
    • Media (e.g. audio and video, media players)
    • Navigation and Structure (e.g. titling, landmarks and headings)
    • Color, Content, Timing and Layout
    • Tabular Data Tables
    • Links, Forms and Accessible Error Feeback
    • ARIA Widgets and Common Web Widgets desgin patterns (e.g based on ARIA Authoring Practices)
    • Images and SVG Graphics Accessibilty
