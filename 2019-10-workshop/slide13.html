Beckwith Program at Nugent Hall: Student Inclusion

  • Nugent Hall integrates Beckwith Program in to the campus residential dorms with 22 full accessible rooms with overhead lifts and accessible bathrooms.
  • Each new dorm floor has an accessible room to allow for full inclusion of students with disabilities in dorm life.
