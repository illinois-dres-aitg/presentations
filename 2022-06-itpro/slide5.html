Differences between the two extensions

  • Page Structure lists all headings and landmarks on a page. Its purpose is for manual checking of the semantic structure of a page.
    • Does the page title accurately describe what is found on the page?
    • Are headings used, and do they properly label the content sections? Are they used in the proper order?
    • Are landmarks used to identify the important regions on a page?
  • SkipTo Landmarks & Headings is an assistive technology. It enables easy and efficient navigation with the page to its headings and important landmark regions.
    • Heading levels displayed are configurable. By default, only h1 through h3 are shown. Through user preferences you can change this to a depth of h2 (minimum) to h6 (maximum depth).
    • Not all landmarks are shown. The extension will alway list the following landmark roles: 'main', 'search', 'navigation', and 'contentinfo'. You can toggle on or off the display of 'complementary' landmarks ('aside' element).
