SkipTo Landmarks & Headings Extension

  • An assistive technology to support keyboard only users in navigating web content based on the landmark regions and heading structure of the page.
  • Shortcut key (Alt+2 or Option+2) to open menu.
  • Organized list of landmark regions on a page.
  • Filtered view of top level heading structure.
  • Selection from the menu moves keyboard focus to the associated landmark or headings.
