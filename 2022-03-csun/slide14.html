Summary

  • Providing a mechanism to allow the user to bypass blocks of content has been an accessibility requirement from the early days of the web.
  • Little has changed regarding the techniques used to satisfy the requirement over the past 20 years, while much has changed in the technologies of the web.
  • SkipTo is a major advancement in implementing the WCAG 2.4.1 Bypass Blocks of Content requirement.
  • SkipTo Landmarks & Headings browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox
