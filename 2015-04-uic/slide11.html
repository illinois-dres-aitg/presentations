First Steps at University of Illinois at Urbana/Champaign

  • Working with specific web developers on campus to bring their web resources into WCAG 2.0 compliance:
    • Disability Services
    • IT Accessibility
    • College of Applied Health Studies
    • Library
    • Office of Public Affairs
    • Public radio and television station
  • Verifying the accessibility features of common web development libraries (e.g. bootstrap.js and jquery-ui) and adding supplemental scripts to improve accessibility
  • Testing and validating the OpenAjax rules (Rule Issue Tracker)
  • Improving the utility and usability of the open source tools by user feedback and feature requests
