ARIA for HTML form controls

  • aria-labelledby for labeling form controls
  • aria-label for labeling form controls
  • aria-required for indicating a form control is required
  • aria-invalid for indicating a form control has an invalid value or format
  • aria-describedby for providing information on an error and how to fix the problem
