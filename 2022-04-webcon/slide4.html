Keyboard Support: Testing

  • Move your mouse away from your work station or cover your track pad to allow your attention to be only on using the keyboard.
    Note: Recommend using Firefox since it's default focus styling is less apparent than Chrome or Safari.
  • Use the tab key to navigate to links, form controls and other interactive content on the page.
  • Questions:
    • Can I tell where keyboard focus is on the page?
    • Using the Tab can I navigate to all the links and form controls on the page.
    • Can I activate links, buttons, checkboxes and radio buttons using the Space or Enter keys?
    • Does the navigation of links and form controls seem predictable?
    • Does focus ever disappear and then magically reapear?
