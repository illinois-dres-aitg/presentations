Keyboard Support: Sequential Navigation and Focus Visibility

  1. All functionality of a web page can be accomplished with the keyboard alone:
    • Sequentially moving through the links and form controls on a web page.
    • Activating links, buttons and changing the states of checkboxes and radio buttons.
  2. The visual indication of focus must be easily identified:
    • An outline appears around the link or form control/label when it has focus.
    • Keyboard focus must always be on a visible element.
  3. Bypass Blocks feature:
    • SkipTo Menu Button
    • "Skip To Main" link
