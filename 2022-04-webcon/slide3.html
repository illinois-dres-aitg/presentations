Keyboard Support: Focus Visibility

  • Default focus styling of links and form controls:
    • Vary between browsers.
    • Often disabled through "reset" stylesheets.
  • Best practices for focus styling:
    • Focus outline of at least 2 pixels in width.
    • At least 2 pixels of padding between outline the text of a link or borders of a form control.
    • Outline includes the label for checkboxes and radio buttons.
    • Outline meets WCAG color contrast requirements with background content.
  • GOAL: When looking at a web page for the first time it should be visually easy to identify the element with keyboard focus and to follow changes in focus.

NOTE: When I test a website and keyboard focus is clearly vsiible and has been intergrated into the look and feel of the website, I am more confident other accessibility features have been addressed.
